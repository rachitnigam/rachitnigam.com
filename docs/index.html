Biography

Rachit Nigam is a PhD student studying computer science at Cornell University. He is a part of the CAPRA research group and is advised by Adrian Sampson. His research interests include programming languages and systems design. He previously worked in the PLASMA research group at the University of Massachusetts Amherst with Arjun Guha.
